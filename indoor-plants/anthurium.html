Anthurium

Lucky plants Symbol of hospitality

Anthuriums are tropical plants known for their lush, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant flower spikes. Native to tropical regions of Central and South America, these stunning plants have become increasingly popular houseplants. Adding anthuriums to your home or office provides numerous benefits beyond their visual appeal.

Plant Attributes

  • Botanical Name:Herbaceous Perennial
  • Mature Size: 2-3 ft. tall and 1-2 ft. wide
  • Sun Exposure: Partial
  • Soil Type: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Soil pH: Neutral to Acidic

Care Tips

    Light: Bright indirect light. Soil: Use a well-draining potting Water: Water when the top two inches of soil are dry. Temperature and Humidity: This plant prefers warm temperatures, seventy degrees, and at least 50 percent humidity. Fertilizer: Fertilize with a foliage houseplant fertilizer during the growing season.
