Dracaena

Known as house plant. Appreciated for their foliage, which is often variegated.

The Dracaena Massangeana, commonly known as the corn plant, isn't just a pretty face in the corner of your room; it's a veritable air purifier. This plant is like a silent, leafy warrior in the battle against indoor air pollutants, such as formaldehyde and xylene, which it absorbs with ease

Plant Attributes

  • Botanical Name: Dracaena spp
  • Mature Size: 2-10 ft. tall
  • Sun Exposure: Indirect
  • Soil Type: Well-drained loamy soil
  • Soil pH: Neutral to Acidic

Care Tips

    Light: Bright indirect light. Soil: Use a well-draining potting Water: Water when the top two inches of soil are dry. Temperature and Humidity: This plant prefers warm temperatures, seventy degrees, and at least 50 percent humidity. Fertilizer: Fertilize with a foliage houseplant fertilizer during the growing season.
